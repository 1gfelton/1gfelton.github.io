I am a student and researcher at Carnegie Mellon University where I am currently pursuing my MS in Computational Design.

My work involves using technology to advance design practices by exploring new possibilities afforded through programming and machine learning.

Prior to my work as a computational designer, I completed my BS, Arch at the University of Utah. After that I worked as an architect in training for Think, AEC where I was tasked with managing two large residential developments on both the construction side and the documentation side.

Outside of my work as a designer, I enjoy skiing, reading, and drinking coffee.
